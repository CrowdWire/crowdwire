CrowdWire: a new way to see the news


Crowdwire was conceptualized by four students as a way to fill in a gap with current social news networks. The vision was to create a newsfeed that anyone could contribute to. It would serve people locally. But the the power of the tool would be the map-interface which would allow people to see topographically where the news was happening. They would also have the opportunity to contribute by uploading stories and photos of their own and tagging them. As more people add to the feed patterns will begin to emerge as to where the news is happening, what is trending, and how geographical associations affect those trends. Car accident? You can see where people are finding associated traffic conge stion. Local event? You can see where local businesses are giving associated discounts. Local emergency? Local responses can give you an idea of what areas are affected. The possibilities are endless. So, the question is...where will you make news today?
