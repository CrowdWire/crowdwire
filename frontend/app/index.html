CROWDWIRE: A NEW WAY TO SEE THE NEWS

Four students created Crowdwire. They wanted to change how news was consumed. They wanted to let local people see WHERE the news is.
Instantly.
So they created a powerful news-to-map interface. It's more than photos and captions. The user can see and show exactly where the news is.

One pin is a story...

Multiple pins are patterns...

Patterns make PREDICTIONS. Patterns tell STORIES. Patterns become TOOLS. And tools bring CHANGE.


So...where will you make news today?
