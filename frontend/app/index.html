CrowdWire: a new way to see the news


Four students created Crowdwire. They wanted to change how news was consumed. They wanted to let local people see WHERE the news is.
Instantly.
So they created a powerful map-interface. It's not just the ability to upload a photo or write a caption. It allowed the user to say exactly where the story was by placing a pin.

One pin is a story...

Multiple pins make patterns...

Patterns make predictions. Patterns tell stories. Patterns become tools.


So, the question is...where will you make news today?
